Our Story

Born from a need to pause

At Ember2Essence, we create handmade candles that are more than light — they are symbols of healing, resilience, and ritual. Ember2Essence is a purpose-driven candle brand centered on intentional living, nervous system awareness, and meaningful rituals.

Each candle is handmade with care, using premium wax, a wooden wick, and a reusable handmade clay jar. Beyond the product itself, we nurture emotional connection with our customers through small rituals that encourage wellbeing, self-reflection, and restoration.

Founder
Ember2Essence was born from the founder's personal experience with sensory overwhelm, emotional fatigue, and the need for grounding rituals in daily life. Lighting a candle began as a small but powerful ritual of pause — a way to slow down, reconnect, and restore balance. That personal practice evolved into a brand built around healing, restoration, and intentional moments of calm.

Candle Care

A better burn

✂️ Before lighting

  • Place on a heat-resistant surface.
  • Keep away from drafts and flammable objects.

🔥 While burning

  • Let the first burn reach the edges.
  • Burn for up to 2–3 hours at a time.
  • Never leave unattended.

♻️ After use

  • Stop when about 10mm of wax remains.
  • Clean and reuse your clay jar.

🕯️ A Better Candle Experience

A few simple steps will help your Ember2Essence candle burn cleaner, last longer, and deliver its full fragrance.

✂️ Before Lighting
  • Trim the wick to 5mm.
  • Place on a heat-resistant surface.
  • Keep away from drafts and flammable objects.
🔥 While Burning
  • Allow the first burn to reach the edges.
  • Burn for up to 2–3 hours.
  • Never leave unattended.
♻️ After Use
  • Stop using when about 10mm of wax remains.
  • Clean and reuse your candle jar.
