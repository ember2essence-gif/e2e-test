A Better Candle Experience

Every Ember2Essence candle is crafted to create moments of warmth and comfort. Following these simple care tips will help you enjoy a cleaner burn, stronger fragrance, and a longer-lasting candle.

✂️

Before Lighting

  • Place your candle on a stable, heat-resistant surface.
  • Keep away from curtains, drafts, moisture and flammable objects.
  • Ensure the wick remains centered.
🔥

While Burning

  • Allow the first burn to create a full melt pool.
  • Burn for no more than 2–3 hours at a time.
  • Never leave a burning candle unattended.
  • Keep away from children and pets.
  • Do not move the candle while wax is liquid.
🕯️

Before You Relight

  • Remove any wick debris.
  • Re-center the wick if needed.
  • Always trim the wick before lighting again.
♻️

After Your Candle

Once around 10mm of wax remains, discontinue use.

Place warm water in the jar to soften the remaining wax, clean thoroughly, and reuse it as a planter, pen holder, or decorative container.

🕯️ A Better Candle Experience

A few simple steps will help your Ember2Essence candle burn cleaner, last longer, and deliver its full fragrance.

✂️ Before Lighting
  • Trim the wick to 5mm.
  • Place on a heat-resistant surface.
  • Keep away from drafts and flammable objects.
🔥 While Burning
  • Allow the first burn to reach the edges.
  • Burn for up to 2–3 hours.
  • Never leave unattended.
♻️ After Use
  • Stop using when about 10mm of wax remains.
  • Clean and reuse your candle jar.

Handmade ceramic variations
  • Each candle is poured into an individually handmade clay jar. Because of the handmade nature of the ceramics and glazing process, we can't fully control the outcome of every glaze firing.
  • Every jar is individually shaped and glazed, so natural variation in colour, texture, and finish is normal from one piece to the next.
  • Our blue glaze in particular can behave differently in the kiln, sometimes producing markings or variation that only become visible once firing is complete.
  • These characteristics are a feature of handmade ceramics, not a fault — they're part of what makes each jar one of a kind.
  • To make sure you receive a piece we're proud of, every jar is hand-checked before dispatch.
How refunds are paid
